## PART 1 — WHO WE ARE SHOOTING

### The short version
**Fluiconnecto by Manuli** is the *service and distribution* arm of the **Manuli** group. Their business is simple to say and hard to do: they keep heavy machinery running by supplying, assembling and on-the-spot replacing **high-pressure hydraulic hoses, fittings and fluid connectors** — and they do it fast, on site, 24/7. Their global brand line is literally **"WE KEEP IT RUNNING."** That line is the spine of this whole video.

### The hierarchy (get the names right on screen)
- **Manuli / Manuli Hydraulics** — the parent. Founded **1935**, headquartered in **Milan, Italy**. ~80-year-old manufacturer of mission-critical fluid-power solutions. ~2,000–2,500 employees, manufacturing & logistics in ~14 countries, ~€450M turnover.
- **Fluiconnecto by Manuli** — the global *service network* that distributes Manuli products and adds engineering + maintenance services. Operates across **5 continents / ~29 countries**: **140+ Hydroshops, 110+ on-site workshops, 39+ mobile SOS vans.**
- **Manuli Fluiconnecto Emirates Trading LLC / Manuli Fluiconnecto Middle East FZE** — the **UAE operation** we are filming. Began operations **2010**, ~25 staff, 24/7 service across multiple emirates.

> Note: "Designed with Intent. Marketed for Impact." is *Hapx's* tagline. The client's tagline is **"WE KEEP IT RUNNING."** Don't mix them on screen.

### What they actually do (the three service pillars — these are gold for visuals)
1. **Hydroshops** — walk-in branches. Each has its own hose-assembly workshop, a service area, and a warehouse stocking thousands of hydraulic parts. Customers order and collect; staff crimp/assemble hoses on demand.
2. **On-Site Workshops** — Fluiconnecto sets up a workshop *inside the customer's facility* (mine, port, plant) so repairs never leave the site.
3. **SOS Vans (Service On-Site)** — fully-equipped mobile workshops that drive to the breakdown — an excavator on a construction site, a crane at a port — and replace/repair the hose on the spot. 24/7, 365 days. This is the most cinematic asset they have.

Additional capabilities to weave in: tube bending, oil filtration, hydraulic lines on new equipment, component overhaul, application engineering, and a 24/7 webshop.

### Who they serve (industries = b-roll variety)
**Construction · Industry & Agriculture · Marine · Mining · Oil & Gas.** The product looks small (a hose), but the consequence is huge: when a hydraulic line fails, a multi-million-dirham machine stops. Fluiconnecto's value proposition is *downtime eliminated.*

### The single most important idea for the film
A hydraulic hose is invisible until it bursts — and then an entire operation halts. Fluiconnecto is the partner who makes sure that moment either never happens, or is over in minutes. **Reliability, speed, safety, partnership.** Sell the stakes (downtime = money lost), then sell the rescue (them).

**Aligned to proposal storyboard:** (1) Company Overview → (2) Core Services & Capabilities → (3) Customer Trust → (4) Global Reach & Local Impact → (5) Close.
